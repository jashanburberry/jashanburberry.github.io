About Burberry

Burberry was founded in 1856 when 21-year-old Thomas Burberry, a former draper's apprentice, opened his own store in Basingstoke, Hampshire, England.[5] By 1870, the business had established itself by focusing on the development of outdoors attire.[5] In 1879, Burberry introduced gabardine to his brand, a hardwearing, water-resistant yet breathable fabric, in which the yarn is waterproofed before weaving.[6] In 1891, Burberry opened a shop in the Haymarket, London.

Burberry Group plc is a British luxury fashion house established in 1856 by Thomas Burberry and headquartered in London, England.[4] It designs and distributes ready to wear, including trench coats, leather accessories, and footwear.
